# Jérémi Montornès

I work as an applied economist at Banque de France.

My research is in wage-setting behaviour, inflation expectations, and input-output models.

## Selected publications

- H. Le Bihan, J. Montornès, T. Heckel, "Sticky wages: evidence from quarterly microeconomic data", *American Economic Journal: Macroeconomics*, 1-32.
- G. Bertola, A. Dabusinskas, M. Hoeberichts, M. Izquierdo, C. Kwapil, and co-authors, "Price, wage and employment response to shocks: evidence from the WDN survey", *Labour Economics* 19(5), 783-791.
- E. Gautier, J. Montornès, "Household Inflation Expectations in France: Lessons from a New Survey and the COVID-19 Crisis", *Economie et Statistique / Economics and Statistics*, 3-19.
- M. B. Khder, J. Montornès, N. Ragache, "Irish GDP Growth in 2015: A Puzzle and Propositions for a Solution", *Economie et Statistique* 517(1), 185-203.
