Research agenda

Empirical work for central bank analysis

Inflation and expectations

Survey-based evidence on household inflation expectations, uncertainty, and the transmission of shocks.

Wage and price dynamics

Microeconomic evidence on nominal rigidities, wage adjustment, and firm-level responses to shocks.

Production networks

Input-output approaches to understand sectoral linkages, reshoring, trade exposure, and aggregate outcomes.

Policy evaluation

Applied work connecting administrative, survey, and macroeconomic data to policy-relevant questions.

Households and firms

Analysis of heterogeneous responses across households, sectors, and firms in France and the euro area.

Central bank communication

Clear dissemination through working papers, policy notes, conference presentations, and public research profiles.
